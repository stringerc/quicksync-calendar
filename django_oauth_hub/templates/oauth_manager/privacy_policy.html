Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ updated_date|default:"January 24, 2025" }}

1. Information We Collect

OAuth Hub ("we," "our," or "us") collects the following information:

  • OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens from connected social media platforms
  • Profile Information: Basic profile data (name, email, profile picture) from connected accounts
  • Connection Logs: Timestamps and status of OAuth connections for security monitoring
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, browser information, and connection attempts for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Facilitate OAuth connections to your social media accounts
  • Maintain secure connections and refresh expired tokens
  • Monitor for unauthorized access attempts
  • Provide customer support when needed
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All OAuth tokens are encrypted using Fernet encryption
  • HTTPS: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing monitoring and security assessments

5. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • OAuth Tokens: Until you disconnect the platform or tokens expire
  • Connection Logs: For 90 days for security monitoring
  • Profile Data: Until you request deletion or disconnect all platforms

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion page)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Disconnect: Remove OAuth connections at any time

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Session management and user authentication
  • CSRF protection during OAuth flows
  • Remembering your connection preferences

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequate country determinations
  • Your explicit consent where required

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of changes.

10. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@oauthhub.com
  • Data Deletion: Request Data Deletion
Compliance: This privacy policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Facebook Platform Policy requirements.
